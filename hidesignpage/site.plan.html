Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to showcase the landscaping services offered by Hi Design. We aim to attract new clients by presenting our portfolio of work, detailing our range of services, and providing an easy way for potential customers to contact us for consultations and quotes. Our goal is to establish a strong online presence that reflects our commitment to quality, creativity, and customer satisfaction in landscape design and maintenance

Audience

Our target audience includes homeowners and commercial property managers in the local and surrounding areas who are looking for professional landscaping services. This includes individuals seeking everything from regular lawn maintenance to complete landscape renovations. We are targeting clients who value high-quality work and are looking for a reliable and experienced landscaping company.

Wireframes

Create three wireframes for your site. One for each page and list them here

Home

The home page wireframe is designed to make a strong first impression. At the top, a clear header contains the company logo and navigation links. Below this, a large hero image will feature one of the stunning landscape photos, with the "Welcome to Hi Design..." text overlaid as a powerful introductory statement. The page will then be divided into clear sections: a brief "About Us" to build trust, a grid showcasing the main services to guide users, and another grid for portfolio highlights to visually prove your expertise. A clear call-to-action button will be present to encourage quote requests. The footer will contain contact details and navigation links for easy access from anywhere on the page

Services

The Services page wireframe focuses on clarity and detail. It will start with the same consistent header. The main content area is organized into a series of sections, one for each service you offer (e.g., Landscape Design, Hardscaping, Maintenance). Each section will have a title, a descriptive paragraph, and a relevant image next to it. This side-by-side layout makes the information easy to digest. This structure allows you to go into detail for each service, helping clients understand the full scope of what Hi Design offers. The page ends with a prominent call-to-action, prompting the user to get in touch for a consultation.

Contact Us

The Contact Us page is designed to be straightforward and user-friendly. The wireframe shows the header at the top for consistency. The page is split into two main columns. On the left, essential contact information like phone number, email, and business hours will be clearly listed. On the right, a simple contact form will allow users to easily send a message or request a quote. The form will include fields for their name, email, phone, and a message. Including an image at the bottom, as you've planned, will keep the page visually engaging and consistent with the rest of the site's branding.
